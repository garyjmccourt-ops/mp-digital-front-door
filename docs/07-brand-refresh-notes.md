# 07 — Brand Refresh Notes

## Direction

The project includes a light digital brand refresh, not a full rebrand.

## Brand principles

- Retain Mermaid Pools’ existing name and brand equity
- Use the supplied Mermaid Pools logo and mermaid silhouette assets
- Explore cleaner digital use of the logo
- Consider separating or simplifying the mermaid figure from the wordmark for digital use
- Retain the water-led blue palette
- Improve digital typography, spacing, contrast and layout
- Create practical usage rules for website and social/profile use

## Visual aim

Premium, modern, trustworthy, practical and clean.

## Guardrail

Do not turn this into a full brand strategy exercise unless separately scoped.
