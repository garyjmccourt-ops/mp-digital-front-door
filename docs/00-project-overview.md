# 00 — Project Overview

## Project

Mermaid Pools Digital Front Door

## Purpose

Create a modern website and customer enquiry foundation that supports Mermaid Pools’ growth objective: increasing qualified customer enquiries and improving visibility of enquiry quality, source, status and conversion.

## Product goal

The product goal is to help Mermaid Pools:

- Present as a premium, modern and trustworthy pool builder
- Better showcase completed projects
- Improve the customer journey from first visit to enquiry
- Increase qualified customer enquiries
- Capture better enquiry information
- Track enquiry status and conversion indicators
- Support basic SEO and search visibility review
- Create a future-ready foundation for CRM, project tracking and AI-guided customer support

## What this is

A first-stage digital front door and enquiry foundation.

## What this is not

This is not a full CRM, customer portal, AI assistant, business operations system or complex marketing automation platform unless separately scoped and approved.
