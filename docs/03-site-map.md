## Page intent

### Home
Present Mermaid Pools as premium, trustworthy and active. Guide visitors to projects, services and enquiry.

### About / Heritage
Build trust through story, experience, local presence and proof.

### Services
Explain the main service areas at a practical level.

### Pool Design and Construction
Primary service page for new pools.

### Renovations
Service page for pool upgrades, refreshes and improvements.

### Projects / Gallery
Showcase completed work and support buyer confidence.

### Process
Explain what happens from enquiry to consultation, design, build and completion.

### FAQs
Answer common customer questions and reduce low-quality enquiries.

### Contact / Enquiry
Capture structured information and route the customer into the agreed follow-up workflow.
