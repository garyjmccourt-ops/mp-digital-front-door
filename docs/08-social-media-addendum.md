# 08 — Social Media Addendum

## Optional setup price

$650

## Purpose

Social media should support reputation, proof of work and project visibility. It should not become Mermaid’s primary sales or customer service channel.

## Setup scope

- Review, setup or reconfiguration of Meta Business Suite where required
- Review or setup of connected Instagram and Facebook business profiles
- Basic profile, bio and contact pathway review
- Alignment of social wording with refreshed website and brand direction
- Setup of a simple monthly content workflow
- Preparation of an initial completed-project posting structure
- Guidance on selecting suitable project photography
- Confirmation that social enquiries are directed back to website, phone or approved email pathway

## Ongoing support options

| Support option | Monthly equivalent | Quarterly billing |
| --- | ---: | ---: |
| Light Support | $90/month | $270/quarter |
| Digital + Social Support | $180/month | $540/quarter |

Digital + Social Support includes basic monthly social media management within the four-hour monthly support allowance.
