# 10 — Assumptions and Exclusions

## Assumptions

- Mermaid Pools will supply logo and brand assets.
- Mermaid Pools will supply or approve project photography.
- Website copy will be drafted, reviewed and approved before launch.
- Domain, hosting and analytics access will be supplied or confirmed during discovery.
- The first-stage build is commercially controlled and MVP-focused.

## Exclusions unless separately approved

- Full CRM
- AI assistant
- Customer portal
- Project tracking system
- Quote tracking
- Task management
- Document storage
- Maintenance tracking
- Payment schedule or invoicing support
- Business platform integrations
- Major photography work
- Major image editing
- Ongoing content management beyond agreed support

## Key guardrail

Do not expand the project into a full CRM, AI assistant, operational platform or complex marketing system unless Gary explicitly approves a scoped variation.
