# 04 — Customer Journey

## Target journey

1. Visitor arrives from search, social, referral or direct visit.
2. Visitor quickly understands Mermaid Pools’ positioning and service fit.
3. Visitor views project proof and service pages.
4. Visitor understands the process and common requirements.
5. Visitor completes a structured enquiry form.
6. Mermaid receives useful qualification information.
7. Enquiry is reviewed, contacted and moved through a simple status workflow.
8. Dashboard/reporting provides visibility of enquiry quality, source and conversion indicators.

## Journey principles

- Make Mermaid feel premium, capable and trustworthy.
- Reduce vague or incomplete enquiries.
- Help visitors self-assess fit without making the process feel cold.
- Keep calls to action clear.
- Make mobile enquiry easy.
- Use photography as proof, not decoration only.
