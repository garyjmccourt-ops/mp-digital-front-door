# Risks and Issues

## Risks

- Delayed access to Mermaid’s photography or brand files
- Unclear domain/hosting access
- Meta Business Suite setup complexity
- Scope creep into full CRM or operational system
- AI being assumed as included when it is optional
- Dashboard expectations becoming too advanced for first-stage scope
- Insufficient project descriptions for gallery/case-study content
- Delays in client review/approval affecting launch timing

## Mitigation

- Use a clear open questions register
- Document all decisions in GitHub
- Separate MVP from Phase 2
- Keep AI and advanced CRM as future addendums
- Confirm dashboard scope early
- Use Mermaid’s supplied assets as the source of truth
